1. **3-Days Masai Mara Camping**

# **The Itinerary**

**Day 1 Nairobi – Masai Mara.**

Depart Nairobi in the morning for a journey south through the floor of the Great Rift Valley,

lunch at Narok. Arrive Mara for late afternoon for game viewing. Dinner and overnight at the preferred campsite.

**Day 2 Masai Mara Full day**.

Full day in the park in search of the Big Five. Meals and overnight at the campsite. (Optional visit the Masai Village in the afternoon)

**Day 3 Masai Mara – Nairobi.**

Early morning breakfast, with optional Masai village visit. drive back to Nairobi via Narok town for Lunch. Arrive in Nairobi around 3:30pm.

1. **4-Days Masai Mara/Lake Nakuru Camping Safari**

# **The Itinerary**

**DAY 1 Nairobi/ Masai Mara**

8:00 A.M Depart from Nairobi to Maasai Mara Game Reserve with a stopover at the great rift valley viewpoint. Arrive in Masai Mara late afternoon, and proceed for a game drive, dinner and overnight stay at the camp.

**DAY 2 Masai mara full day**

Early breakfast, full day exploring this unique park which really forms the northern part of the famous Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at camp.

**DAY 3 Masai mara/Lake Nakuru**

Early breakfast, with optional Masai village visit. Departure for Lake Nakuru with game drive enroute and Picnic lunch. Arrive Nakuru early evening dinner and overnight at budget hotel.

**DAY 4 Day Lake Nakuru/Nairobi**

Early breakfast, proceed for a game drive to Lake Nakuru world renowned for its masses of flamingoes often referred to as the “Pink Lake”. Beside birds, it is possible to see small game, like Water Buck, Impala, Rhino and Buffalo. After lunch depart to Nairobi arriving around 4/5pm

# **Tour Overview**

Discover the Masai Mara National Reserve and Lake Nakuru National Park on a 4‑day budget safari featuring daily game drives, abundant wildlife, and the chance to witness the Big Five along with iconic sights as the Great Mara Migration. Optional Cultural experiences such as the Maasai village visit, hot‑air balloon safari. Explore Lake Nakuru’s rich habitats—home to flamingos, endangered rhinos, Rothschild giraffes and diverse birdlife

1. **7-Days Masai Mara /Lake Nakuru/Amboseli Camping**

# **Tour Overview**

Embark on an epic budget adventure through Kenya’s top wildlife reserves, beginning with the stunning Great Rift Valley, continue to the Masai Mara in search of the Big Five and lastly, Amboseli National Park at the foot of Mount Kilimanjaro, offering breathtaking mountain views and sightings of large elephant herds.

**The ItineraryDay 1: Nairobi/ Masai Mara**Leave Nairobi for Masai Mara Game Reserve. Picnic lunch enroute. On arrival proceed for a game drive in search of the big five. Return to the campsite for dinner and overnight stay. **Day 2: Masai Mara Game Drive**A full day spent game viewing across the rich, tree studded grassland and rolling hills. The famous mara is renowned for its great herds of plain game and the Big 5. All meals and overnight stay in the campsite. **Day 3: Masai Mara Full day**Game viewing in the Mara with an optional nature walk or a visit to a Maasai village in between meal breaks. Dinner and overnight stay at the campsite.

# **Day 4**

**Masai Mara/ Lake Nakuru**

After early game drive leave Masai Mara for Nakuru National Park. Picnic lunch enroute. Dinner and overnight at a hotel.

**Day 5: Nakuru/ Amboseli**

After breakfast, morning game drive. Have lunch in Nairobi and proceed to Amboseli National Park. Once in the park proceed for a short game drive. Dinner and overnight at the campsite.

**Day 6: Amboseli National Reserve**

Full day in Amboseli with an optional tour to Maasai village, Meals and night at the campsite.

**Day 7: Amboseli/Nairobi**

After breakfast leave campsite for the last game drive as you leave the park for Nairobi. Picnic lunch served enroute. Arrive in Nairobi late afternoon.
